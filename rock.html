Classic Rock

Rock music is a genre characterized by its heavy emphasis on electric guitars, bass, drums, and often energetic vocals. It originated in the mid-20th century in the United States, drawing from a blend of blues, rhythm and blues, and country music. Rock is known for its driving rhythms, catchy melodies, and rebellious attitude, making it a powerful force in popular culture. Over the decades, rock has evolved into various subgenres including classic rock, punk rock, alternative rock, metal, and more, each with its own distinct sound and aesthetic.
