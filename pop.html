Pop

Pop music, short for popular music, is a genre characterized by its broad appeal to a wide audience. It typically features catchy melodies, simple song structures, and repetitive lyrics. Pop music often incorporates elements from other genres such as rock, R&B, electronic, and hip-hop, making it versatile and adaptable to current trends. It's known for its emphasis on hooks and memorable choruses, making it easy to sing along to and suitable for radio play. Pop music has been a dominant force in the music industry for decades, producing numerous chart-topping hits and influential artists who shape popular culture.
